ABOUT

The Pillai Group of Institutions stands as one of Maharashtra’s leading
educational networks, dedicated to excellence in academics, innovation,
and holistic development. With over 50 years of legacy in education, the
group has built a strong reputation for nurturing talent across diverse
disciplines, from engineering and management to arts, science, and
commerce.

Under the vision of Dr. K. M. Vasudevan Pillai, the institution has continually
evolved with the changing times, empowering thousands of students every
year to grow into confident professionals and change makers.

The group's campuses equipped with modern infrastructure and an
inclusive culture are home to some of the most active student communities
in the state. Events like EUFORIA embody the spirit of Pillai: creativity,
collaboration, and community engagement.





--- PAGE page_03 ---
10 YEARS OF

Pillai EUFORIA is the annual cultural festival of the Pillai Group of Institutions,
celebrating a decade of creativity, expression, and togetherness. Over the
years, EUFORIA has evolved into one of Mumbai's most recognized college
festivals, a space where talent meets opportunity and energy meets
purpose.

From live concerts and dance battles to art installations and celebrity
performances, EUFORIA brings an experience that unites thousands of
students, artists, and brands under one celebration.

Marking its 10th edition, EUFORIA proudly carries the tagline “A Decade of
Celebration Redefined,” symbolizing not just a milestone, but a renewed
spirit of innovation and cultural impact.
